July 23 - Written By Dylan Bosch


First Blog



If you've somehow found yourself reading this: Welcome! I'll keep this entry short.

I've never tried blogging before. The idea always seemed a little futile - who's going to read the blog of a 23 year old aspiring
photographer? There's millions of other blogs and websites to check out. But I'm pretty bored so I figured why not give it a shot?

I don't really have a plan for what I'd like to write about; maybe my daily life, my journey to find a job, or maybe about my hobbies and
interests - who knows? I don't want to put too much planning into it, I'd rather it be more of a natural thing.

If you made it this far I hope you'll come back and read entry #2 when it gets written. In the meantime, it's Saturday, it's beautiful out, and
life is pretty good. Time to tackle the day.
